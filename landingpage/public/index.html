Privacy By Design

In an era where your attention is monetized and your data is harvested, Portalific takes a fundamentally different approach.

Portalific runs entirely in your browser. Your personal data stays on your device by default, not on someone else's server.

When you do opt to use synchronization between devices, your data is encrypted using strong AES-GCM encryption from browser crypto standards before it ever leaves your device.

No tracking, no algorithms trying to maximize your "engagement," and no third-party analytics. Just the content you choose to follow, presented the way you want to see it.
